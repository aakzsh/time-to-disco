Rules to Play Time to Disco



Hit the Play Button on HomePage


Then you can select any song of your choice from the given list


Hit the continue Button


Now the game will start. The song will play for 30 seconds. And meanwhile you have to sync to it using the dance pad buttons. It's like a virtual form of dance revolution. Whenever any button turns red, you have to tap it to make it green. Your scores will be calculated based on your speed and accuracy, and will be displayed after the song end


Have fun playing the game
